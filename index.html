How to Use

Nila Hair Care Oil

To use Nila Hair Care Oil, shake the bottle well and pour a small amount into your palm. Massage the oil into your scalp for 5-10 minutes, then apply it to the length of your hair, focusing on the ends. Leave the oil in for at least 30 minutes or overnight for deeper conditioning. Rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water and wash with a mild shampoo, possibly shampooing twice to remove all the oil. Follow with a conditioner for added softness and shine. Use 2-3 times a week for the best results..
